Study Instructions

The purpose of this study is to explore how people can use subtle gestures to query auditory information and obtain results. In particular, we aim to understand which word or part of the audio a subtle gesture is referring to at the moment it is performed.

Please imagine that as soon as you make a gesture, the system immediately returns a result. At that moment, think about which word or expression you wanted to ask about.

Procedure

  1. You will watch a video that is approximately 5 minutes long.
  2. While watching the video, press the SPACE bar whenever you feel that you want the system to do something for you (e.g., look something up, clarify something).
  3. Please try to press the space bar at least five times per video.

After Watching the Video

After the video ends, you will be shown:

  • Short video clips covering 3 seconds around each moment when you pressed the space bar, and
  • The corresponding transcript for each clip.

For each clip, you will be asked to:

  1. Select the word or phrase you were targeting at that moment, and
  2. Describe what you wanted to ask or know about it.

Attention Notice

  • After watching each video, you will be asked to complete a short quiz about the video content.
  • Please watch the video carefully and pay attention throughout, as the quiz will assess your understanding of what you have watched.

Session Structure

  • This process will be repeated for a total of four videos.
  • You may take breaks at any time.
  • You are free to stop participating at any point if you wish.
Remember: There is no right or wrong timing. Press the SPACE bar whenever it feels natural to you to interrupt and ask the system for help.

Thank you for your participation.
